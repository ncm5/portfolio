Nicholas Chua

Product Designer

Hi there, I'm Nicholas. I’m a product designer with a background in multimedia design, driven by a passion for crafting meaningful, user-centered experiences. My journey started in graphic design and video editing, where I spent 3 years honing my storytelling skills and developing a strong eye for detail. Now, I’m excited to bring that experience to UX/UI, blending design and front-end skills to create clean, functional layouts.

Most recently, I worked as a multimedia designer across graphic design, video editing, and UI/UX projects, which sparked my interest in the tech space. As the sole designer and editor, I wore many hats, learning to adapt quickly in a fast-paced, startup-driven environment. My work contributed to enhanced sales and brand perception, as I gained insight into various industries and their design needs. My approach centers on functional minimalism, simplifying and enhancing user experiences.

Figma is my go-to tool for designing screens, creating prototypes, and building cohesive design systems. I’m always on the lookout for projects that combine creativity and functionality. When I’m not designing, I enjoy diving into creative and technical hobbies, from 3D design to tinkering with electronics. Cooking, playing sports, and exploring new skills are my favorite ways to recharge and stay inspired.
