Warisan Party

The landing page for Warisan Party is designed to inspire trust, unity, and progress. Featuring a clean and modern layout, it highlights the party's mission, vision, and key initiatives with strong visual appeal. A prominent hero section showcases the party's slogan alongside an image of its leader or a unifying symbol.

Political Accessible Design Modern

Design Process

Research & Analysis

The project began with research into the political landscape of Malaysia and the values associated with Warisan Party. We analyzed competitors' websites to identify best practices and gain insights into effective design elements for political platforms. Audience profiling helped define key user personas, including voters, volunteers, and activists, ensuring the design would meet their expectations.

Visual Identity & Branding

The visual design was aligned with the Warisan Party’s brand identity. A patriotic color palette inspired by Malaysia’s flag—reds, blues, and whites—was chosen to reflect a sense of national pride. Rounded and open typography conveyed approachability, while bold headings emphasized strength and confidence.

User Interface Design
  • Hero Section: The page opens with a powerful hero image or video, featuring the party’s slogan alongside a leader’s image or cultural symbolism to establish an immediate emotional connection.
  • Values Section: The core values of Warisan Party are presented as visually distinct blocks, making them easy to scan while reinforcing the party’s commitment to equality, economic growth, cultural harmony, and community welfare.
  • Latest Updates: News, announcements, and upcoming events are displayed in a clean grid or carousel layout, allowing users to stay informed.
  • Call-to-Actions (CTAs): Prominent CTAs like “Join Us,” “Donate,” and “Volunteer” are strategically placed throughout the page to maximize engagement.
Accessibility & Responsiveness

Special attention was given to ensuring the design met accessibility standards, with high color contrast, readable text, and responsive design that adapts seamlessly to mobile devices and tablets. Iconography and images were selected to represent inclusivity and national pride, reinforcing Warisan Party’s message of unity.

Development & Optimization

The landing page was designed with scalability and performance in mind. Optimized image sizes and lazy loading techniques ensured fast loading speeds, while lightweight CSS and JavaScript frameworks allowed smooth interactions.

Key Takeaways

The final design successfully encapsulates the values and mission of Warisan Party in a visually appealing, functional, and accessible format. It encourages interaction, fosters community involvement, and positions the party as forward-thinking and approachable.

This project underscored the importance of blending strong visual identity with functionality in political web design. Through thoughtful research, user-centered design, and technical execution, the Warisan Party landing page effectively achieves its goal of connecting with the Malaysian audience and promoting its message of unity and progress.
